Lasagna

  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomato Pasta Sauce, 3 bottles (500ml)
  • White Pasta Sauce
  • Carrots Sliced
  • Broccoli
  • Mince Meat of Choice: Chicken or Lamb
  • Onion
  • Crushed Garlic

Steps

  • Preheat oven at 180C Fan Forced
  • First cook your choice of meat with onion and garlic
  • Once cooked, put tomato pasta sauce and heat the sauce for a few minutes
  • Place the lasagna sheets in a tray and put some sauce on the sheets
  • Place vegetable and meat ingredients on the sheets, put a little bit more of the tomato sauce, then place another set of sheets on top and repeat.
  • Once at the 3rd set of sheets, place the white pasta sauce on top and then top it off with mozzarella cheese.
  • Place the tray into the oven and time for 45 minutes
  • Once done, leave it out for a few minutes to rest and then cut and serve.
