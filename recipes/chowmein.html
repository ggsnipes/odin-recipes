Chicken Chow Mein

Ingredients

  • Hokkien Noodles
  • Chicken Thighs Diced
  • Capsicum
  • Celery
  • Soy Sauce
  • Onion
  • Crushed Garlic
  • Chinese Cabbage

Steps

  • First cook the onion and garlic for a few minutes
  • Put in the chicken thighs and cook them till close to done
  • Put in the vegetables and cook them through with sauces and ensure it's done well
  • Cook the noodles through boiling water and drain it after 5-10 minutes
  • Once drained, use sesame oil to keep it loose and not stick
  • Serve together and mix well
  • Eat and Enjoy!
